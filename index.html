Rules & Guidelines

Please read the following rules carefully to ensure a smooth and fair competition.

Eligibility & Teams

  • Open to UG & PG students from all colleges.
  • Team size must be 3 to 5 members.
  • All team members must belong to the same college (inter-departmental teams are welcome).

Event Day

  • Participants must attend in formal attire.
  • Bring your own necessary resources (laptops, chargers, software, etc.).
  • Complimentary meals and refreshments will be provided to all participants throughout the event.

Judging & Fair Play

Hackathon Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity20 points
    • Originality of the idea, Uniqueness of approach
  • Technical Implementation20 points
    • Code quality & architecture, Use of tools/tech stack
  • Impact & Usefulness20 points
    • Relevance to business or society, Scalability potential
  • User Experience & Design15 points
    • UI/UX quality, Ease of use, Visual appeal
  • Presentation & Communication15 points
    • Clarity of pitch, Demo effectiveness, Q&A
  • Time Management & Completion10 points
    • Working prototype readiness, Progress made

✅ Total: 100 points

  • All solutions must be software-based. No hardware components are permitted in the final judging.
  • Plagiarism or any form of academic dishonesty will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Teams retain full ownership (IP) of what they build.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

UG and PG students from any college. Teams (3–5 members) must belong to the same college; inter-department teams are allowed.

Is registration free?

Yes, registration is completely free for all participants.

What do we need to upload at registration?

You need to submit your team details and a PPT outlining your project idea (please use the official PPT template). Registration with PPT must be completed by 13-Oct-2025.

Is internet/access to cloud provided?

Yes, internet access will be provided at the venue. Any specific cloud credit details will be shared closer to the event date.
